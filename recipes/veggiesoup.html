Vegetable Soup

Description

Lovely vegetable soup recipe

Ingredients

  • Vegetables
  • Herbs
  • Stock
  • Spices
  • Salt
  • Coriander to garnish

Steps

  1. Chop some vegetables
  2. Put the vegetables in a bowl and cover with water
  3. Boil for 30 mins
  4. Add some flavourings
  5. Stick in the blender
